Frequently Asked Questions

Is my body data stored anywhere?
No. All analysis runs locally in your browser and is never sent to or stored on any server. Your measurements disappear when you close the tab.
How accurate are the results?
FITME provides comparative analysis based on statistical golden ratio benchmarks. Results are for reference and style guidance only — not medical diagnosis or professional advice.
Unit conversion isn't working.
Click the Units button in the header to manually set metric or imperial. If the issue persists, email us with your browser and device details.
I have a question about an ad shown on FITME.
Ads on FITME are served by Google AdSense. For ad content issues, you can report directly to Google or notify us and we'll review it.
How do I delete my data?
FITME does not store personal data on servers. To clear locally stored language and unit preferences, clear your browser's site data in browser settings.
