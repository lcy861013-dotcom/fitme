About FITME

FITME is independently built and maintained by Changyong Lee, a solo founder based in Ansan, South Korea.

Why I Built FITME: I struggled to find clothes that fit my proportions. Standard S/M/L sizes ignored my torso-to-leg ratio and shoulder width. After researching fit systems like Kibbe Body Types and ISO 8559 garment standards, I built this tool to share what I learned.

Project Started: March 2026

Contact: Contact page · lcy861013@gmail.com

What you will find on FITME

  • Free proportion tool — height, weight, and waist in your browser; optional measurements for sharper fit guidance. Not a medical test or tailor’s chart.
  • Original fit guides — 25+ English articles on pants fit, WHR, hand-span measuring, and styling by body shape, with localized editions in Japanese and Portuguese.
  • Transparent publishing — methodology limits, update policy, and a named founder (no anonymous “team” pages).
