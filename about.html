About FITME

FITME is a free body proportion analysis platform. Enter three measurements — height, weight, and waist — and get an instant breakdown of your body ratios, body type classification, and personalized style recommendations.

Why We Built This

Most people shop for clothes by size — S, M, L, XL. But size tells you almost nothing about fit. Two people can wear the same size and have completely different proportions: different shoulder-to-waist ratios, different leg-to-torso ratios, different hip widths. The result? Clothes that technically fit but never look quite right.

FITME was built to solve this. By understanding your actual body proportions — not just your size — you can make smarter decisions about what styles will work for your shape, which cuts to avoid, and how to build outfits that consistently look intentional and proportional.

How It Works

You enter three numbers: height, weight, and waist circumference. From these, FITME calculates your key body ratios — including your waist-to-height ratio, estimated shoulder and hip proportions, and leg-to-torso balance. The result is a body type profile with specific style guidance tailored to your proportions.

The analysis is instant, completely free, and requires no account or registration. Your data is not stored.

What You Get

BODY TYPE

Understand your proportional shape — whether you're closer to inverted triangle, hourglass, rectangle, or pear — based on actual ratios, not guesswork.

STYLE GUIDE

Specific recommendations for what fits, what cuts to prioritize, and what silhouettes to avoid for your body type.

PROPORTION SCORE

A numerical breakdown of your key ratios so you understand exactly where you stand and what direction to go.

FREE & INSTANT

No signup, no payment, no waiting. Enter your numbers and get your results immediately.

Our Approach

FITME is built around one principle: data over guesswork. The fashion industry has conditioned people to rely on "does this look good?" as the primary filter for clothing decisions. But that question is distorted by lighting, mirrors, mood, and marketing. The better question is "does this match my proportions?" — and that question has a factual answer.

By grounding style decisions in actual measurements, FITME helps you build a consistent, reliable foundation for how you dress. The goal isn't to make everyone look the same — it's to help each person understand what works specifically for them.

Our Blog

All content is free and written to be practically useful — not generic advice, but specific guidance you can apply immediately.
