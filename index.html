Perfect Fit Me (FITME)는 옷 사이즈(S/M/L)가 아니라 체형 비율을 이해하도록 돕는 무료 교육 사이트입니다. 키·몸무게·허리를 입력하면 WHR, 다리·몸통 비율, 체형 프로필과 스타일 방향을 추정합니다.

FITME는 사진을 업로드하지 않으며, 3D 스캔·의료 조언·수선 치수를 대체하지 않습니다. 측정값은 브라우저에서만 처리됩니다. 25편 이상의 원문 핏 가이드(바지 핏, 어깨, 캡슐 워드로브, 측정법)를 1인 창업자가 직접 작성·업데이트합니다.

소개(방법론·한계), 작동 방식, 콘텐츠 기준을 참고하세요. 추천 글: 비율에 맞는 바지, WHR과 청바지 허리 뜸, 2XL·오버핏 직구 핏.

왜 만들었나요

많은 사람이 화면 속 사이즈표만 보고 삽니다.
그 표는 당신의 실루엣을 설명하지 않습니다.

리뷰 사진과 사이즈표가 같아도 어깨·허리·다리 비율이 다르면 같은 옷이 전혀 다른 핏이 됩니다. 그래서 반품·교환을 반복하게 되죠.

FITME는 “내 비율이 먼저” 보이도록 만들었습니다. 숫자 몇 개로 실루엣과 스타일 방향을 정리하면 — 어떤 넥라인·실루엣을 고르고 무엇을 피할지 — 직구와 국내 온라인 쇼핑이 훨씬 덜 막막해집니다.

저는 매장보다 온라인 쇼핑을 훨씬 많이 해요. 키 175·78kg · 상의 2XL(오버핏) · 허리 32 · 발 250mm — 요즘 유행도 그렇고 배 편하려고 2XL 많이 입어요. 그런데 사이즈표만 보면 “거의 맞겠지” 싶었거든요 — 사진·리뷰 보고 집에서 입어 보면 “거의 맞는데… 나한테는 아닌” 그 느낌이 반복됐어요. 예쁜 옷인데 실루엣이 안 맞는, 그 패턴요.

트레이닝복 하나가 맞을 때까지 두세 벌은 더 사 봤고, 반품 박스는 쌓였죠. 그때 깨달았어요 — 사이즈표가 아니라 어깨·다리·허리 비율을 모르고 있었다는 걸요.

WHY FITME?

Why Proportions Matter More Than Size

Two people can wear the same size and look completely different. The reason isn't the clothes — it's body proportions. Shoulder width, leg-to-torso ratio, waistline definition: these are what actually control how clothing looks on you. FITME measures yours precisely, without photos.

"Your size is a label. Your proportions are the truth."

"사이즈는 그냥 태그입니다. 비율이 진짜입니다."

📐

Proportions, Not Size Labels

S, M, L sizes tell you almost nothing about fit. Two people wearing the same size can have completely different shoulder widths, different leg-to-torso ratios, different waist-to-hip curves. That gap — between label and proportion — is exactly why clothes that technically fit often look wrong. FITME bypasses size labels entirely. Enter 3 measurements and get a precise breakdown of your actual body ratios, so you can dress for your proportions instead of a tag.

📐

Mathematical Precision

FITME applies the Golden Ratio (φ = 1.618) — the mathematical principle behind classical art and natural beauty — alongside Waist-to-Hip Ratio (WHR), shoulder-to-waist differential, and vertical proportion indices to generate your personalized FITME DNA Score™ (0–100). Unlike photo assessments that vary by lighting, angle, and camera quality, our measurement-based algorithm delivers consistent, reproducible results every time. Your body's true proportions don't change based on camera angle — and neither does your FITME score.

✨

Enhance Strengths, Conceal Weaknesses

Every body has natural strengths — proportions that, when dressed correctly, create a visually powerful silhouette. The challenge is knowing what your strengths actually are. FITME provides objective data about your unique proportions: leg-to-torso ratio, shoulder-to-hip width, and waistline definition. Armed with this data, you make intentional styling choices: A-line skirts to balance wider hips, structured shoulders to create breadth, high-waist cuts to elongate the leg line, vertical patterns to add height. These are data-driven strategies from your specific measurements — not generic body-type advice.

Horizontal Balance

Vertical Structure

Vertical Proportions

Your leg-to-torso ratio dictates where your waistline should sit. People with shorter torsos benefit from mid-rise cuts, while those with longer torsos can use high-waisted styles to elongate their silhouette.

Math Layer

The Golden Ratio

Classical aesthetics often refer to the golden ratio (1:1.618) as a benchmark for balance. We compare your measurements to these statistical averages to identify your unique strengths and styling opportunities.

Why Measurements Over Photos?

Photos can be deceptive due to lens distortion, camera angles, and lighting. Numbers, however, don't lie. By using precise measurements, FITME provides a 100% private, objective analysis that works regardless of how you're posing or what you're currently wearing. This data-first approach ensures that your styling recommendations are based on your actual physical structure, providing a reliable blueprint for your wardrobe.

🤚 Measure with Hand Spans — Your Hand is Always a Ruler

No tools at all? One hand is all you need. Once you know your hand span, you carry a ruler everywhere.

STEP 01 — Find Your Hand Span
👨 Adult Male Average 20–22 cm
👩 Adult Female Average 17–19 cm
✋ How to Measure Accurately

Spread your hand wide — thumb tip to pinky tip. Press against a wall, mark with a pencil, then measure. That's your personal hand span.

🧬 Hidden Body Proportion Laws

Ancient formulas da Vinci also discovered — useful for estimating body parts without a tape measure.

🦶
Forearm length = Foot length
Your elbow-to-wrist length matches your foot size with surprising accuracy — usually within 1cm.
🔄
Neck × 2 ≈ Waist circumference
Measure your neck and double it to estimate your waist. More accurate for average body types.
↔
Arm span ≈ Height
Da Vinci's Vitruvian Man key insight. Fingertip-to-fingertip with arms spread equals your height.
✨
Navel height = Height × 61.8%
The golden ratio (φ = 1.618) lives in your body. The distance from floor to navel is ~61.8% of your height.
💪
Wrist × 2 ≈ Ankle circumference
Upper and lower body joints are proportional. Knowing your wrist lets you estimate your ankle size.
👋
Thumb length ≈ Nose length
An old folk fact — hold your thumb next to your nose and they're almost exactly the same length.
