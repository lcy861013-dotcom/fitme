Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026
Your body measurement data is never transmitted to or stored on any server. All analysis runs locally in your browser and disappears when you close the tab.

1. Information We Collect

FITME collects the following categories of information:

  • Body measurement data (height, weight, waist, shoulder width, etc.) — entered voluntarily, processed locally in your browser only, never transmitted to our servers.
  • Anonymous usage statistics — collected via Google Analytics (page views, session duration, device type). No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Standard server logs — IP address, browser type, referring URL. Used solely for security and performance monitoring. Not linked to body data.
  • Advertising cookies — placed by Google AdSense for contextual ad delivery. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide body proportion analysis and personalized style recommendations — entirely on your device.
  • To improve service quality using aggregated, anonymous analytics (no individual tracking).
  • To serve contextual advertisements via Google AdSense.

Your body measurements are never used for marketing, advertising targeting, or shared with any third party.

3. Data Storage & Retention

Your body measurements are never stored on FITME servers. All measurement data lives exclusively in your browser's local memory and is automatically deleted when you close the tab or browser. We have zero access to your body data at any time.

Language and unit preferences are saved in your browser's localStorage for convenience and are never transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics — Anonymous visitor statistics. No body data is shared. Google Privacy Policy ↗
  • Google AdSense — Contextual advertising. May use cookies for ad personalization. Google Ads Policy ↗
  • Google Fonts — Font delivery via CDN. No personal data transmitted beyond standard CDN requests.
  • Microsoft Clarity — Anonymous session analytics for UX improvement. No body data is shared.
  • ipapi.co — Used once to detect your country for automatic unit settings (metric/imperial). Only the country code is used; no data is stored.

5. Cookies

FITME uses the following:

  • fitme_lang — stores your language preference (localStorage, your device only).
  • fitme_units — stores your unit preference (localStorage, your device only).
  • Google Analytics / AdSense cookies — third-party cookies for analytics and advertising. You may opt out via your browser settings or Google Analytics Opt-out ↗.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR), you have the right to access, correct, and delete any personal data we hold. Since FITME does not store body measurements on servers, there is no body data to request deletion of. For any other data inquiries, contact us at the address below.

7. Children's Privacy

FITME is intended for users aged 14 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14. If you believe a minor has submitted data, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of FITME after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
