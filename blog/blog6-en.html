5 Basics for a Full Week of Outfits

2026.04.01 · FITME Style Guide

The 5 Essential Pieces

① White round-neck tee (oversized or regular fit) ② Black straight trousers ③ Light-wash denim jacket ④ Khaki / beige chino pants ⑤ Light indigo wide-leg denim

With just these 5 pieces and two pairs of shoes — white sneakers and black loafers — you can build 7 complete, non-repetitive outfits.

Monday — Clean and Sharp

White tee + black straight trousers + black loafers. Clean enough for business casual, relaxed enough for everyday. Lightly tuck in the tee for a more polished look.

Tuesday — Layered and Trendy

White tee + denim jacket + khaki chinos + white sneakers. Denim over khaki is the ultimate earth-tone combination. Leave the denim jacket open for a relaxed, confident vibe.

Wednesday — Effortlessly Put-Together

White tee + wide-leg denim + white sneakers. Wide-leg denim requires the half tuck — just fold the front of the tee inside. Comfortable, proportional, and effortless.

Thursday — Street Smart

White tee + denim jacket + black trousers + black loafers. Black trousers + black loafers in tone-on-tone creates a leg-lengthening effect. The denim jacket punctuates the whole look.

Friday — Light and Free

White tee + khaki chinos + white sneakers. Khaki + white is one of the cleanest natural-color combinations. Cuff the chinos once for a casual finish.

Weekend — Maximum Comfort

Saturday: Wide-leg denim + denim jacket for a full denim look. Use different washes (light jacket, medium pants) so it doesn't read as a matchy set. Sunday: Swap to jogger-style chinos for an ultra-relaxed vibe.

💡 Invest in quality for your basics. A great white tee and black trousers will last years. Uniqlo, COS, and Muji offer excellent quality-to-price ratios for essentials.
